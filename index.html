  • Moomiyo: Moomiyo, a natural adaptogen sourced from the resin of the Shilajit plant, has been used for centuries in traditional medicine. Rich in minerals, vitamins, and antioxidants, Moomiyo combats oxidative stress and inflammation—key factors that can negatively impact cognitive function and hearing health.   • L-Arginine: L-Arginine is an amino acid that improves blood circulation, including to the ears. It converts to nitric oxide, helping relax blood vessels and support healthy ear function. This improved circulation delivers essential nutrients and oxygen to the auditory system. Additionally, L-Arginine promotes overall vitality, enhances exercise performance, and aids recovery, aligning perfectly with NeuroQuiet’s goal to support hearing and cognitive function.
  • L-Tyrosine: L-Tyrosine, another amino acid, supports cognitive function, especially under stress. As a precursor to neurotransmitters like dopamine and norepinephrine, it enhances mood, focus, and mental clarity. Supplementation with L-Tyrosine can improve cognitive performance during stressful times, supporting stress resilience and maintaining a balanced neurotransmitter environment crucial for optimal brain function.
  • Alpha-GPC: Alpha-GPC (Alpha-Glycerylphosphorylcholine) is a potent source of choline, essential for acetylcholine production, a neurotransmitter crucial for memory and learning. Supplementing with Alpha-GPC enhances cognitive function, particularly in aging adults, and may improve memory, focus, and brain performance. It also reduces anxiety and depression symptoms, promoting a balanced mood and cognitive clarity by crossing the blood-brain barrier.
  • GABA: GABA (Gamma-Aminobutyric Acid) regulates mood and induces relaxation by inhibiting excessive neuronal firing. It is a natural remedy for stress and anxiety, and studies show increasing GABA levels enhances tranquility and mental well-being. In NeuroQuiet, GABA promotes a calmer mind, supporting focus and clarity, especially for individuals managing hearing loss or cognitive challenges.
  • L-Dopa Bean: L-Dopa Bean, derived from the Mucuna pruriens plant, enhances dopamine production, a neurotransmitter important for mood regulation and motivation. It may alleviate tinnitus symptoms (ringing in the ears) and improve cognitive function and emotional well-being. L-Dopa Bean supports both auditory health and mental clarity, making it an essential component of NeuroQuiet’s formula.
